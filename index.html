How to play

  • Press the Hit button to add a card to your hand
  • Press the Stand button to stick with your current hand and start the dealer's turn
  • Press the + or - buttons to increase/decrease your bet
  • Press the New Game button to reset available funds back to 500 and start a new game
  • Winning the hand doubles your bet
  • Press the Deal button to deal out new hands
